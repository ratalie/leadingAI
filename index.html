Lessons from My Journey

The good, the messy, and what I wish I knew earlier

What Worked

  • Staying curious and asking "why not?"
  • Building bridges between tech and people
  • Taking calculated risks

What Was Messy

  • Resistance to change (mine and others')
  • Overestimating tech, underestimating culture
  • Learning to let go of perfection

What I Wish I Knew Earlier

  • Start before you're ready
  • Your network is your net worth
  • Technology changes, principles don't

Key Takeaways

  2. Resilience > Stability
